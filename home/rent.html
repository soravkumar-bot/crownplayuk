Average Rent in the UK

Rent is the largest monthly expense for most UK households. Prices vary by city, property type, and location within a city.

Factors Affecting Rent

  • City or town location
  • Property size and condition
  • Proximity to transport
  • Local demand

Ways to Reduce Rent Costs

Sharing accommodation, choosing suburban areas, and negotiating longer rental agreements can help reduce housing expenses.
